🪄 PlayCam — AR Magic Mirror for iOS

PlayCam is a SwiftUI-based iOS app that brings an interactive Augmented Reality photo booth experience to your iPhone. Using ARKit and Vision, 
users can apply real-time face filters, capture their look, and instantly share it via a QR code.
